#light_environmeddntt

<img src={light_environmeddnttIcon} alt="light_environmeddntt icon" style={{height: '80px'}} />

Mesh Entity

Sets the color and angle of the light from the sun and sky. 

Typical setup:
1. Create an <b>env_sky</b> entity to use as your skybox
2. Create a <b>light_environment</b> entity and set <b>Sky IBL Source</b> to the name of the <b>env_sky</b> entity
3. Right-click on your <b>light_environment</b> entity and select 'Selected environment light -&gt; Estimate lighting from HDR skybox'
4. Adjust angle and brightness of the sunlight as you see fit
